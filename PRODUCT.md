# Product

## Register

product

## Users

The developer plus a small trusted circle (friends/family). Each person manages their own recipe collection. Used in casual home cooking contexts — on a laptop in the kitchen, on a phone while shopping, on a tablet while cooking.

## Product Purpose

S-Base is a personal recipe collection manager. It lets users create, organize, rate, and browse their recipes across statuses (to try, success, needs tweak, failure, archived). The recipes module is called "Taste tracker." Success means the user can quickly find, add, or update a recipe without friction.

## Brand Personality

Effective, clean, capable, modern. Quiet confidence — the UI gets out of the way. No marketing flourish, no decoration without purpose.

## Anti-references

No strongly held anti-references.

## Design Principles

1. **Get in, get out.** The primary task is finding or recording a recipe fast. Minimize taps/clicks for every common action.
2. **Dark by default.** The all-dark background is intentional — reduces glare in low-light cooking environments, makes the brand green pop.
3. **One job per screen.** Each page has a clear primary action. Edit is for editing, list is for browsing, detail is for reading.
4. **Mobile is not an afterthought.** Recipe lookups and updates happen on phones as often as on desktops.
5. **Information density with breathing room.** Compact enough to see many recipes at a glance, spacious enough to not feel cramped.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Always-dark mode. WCAG AA contrast target where practical. Reduced motion respected.
